Early depression diagnosis using Artificial Intelligence
The wolves of Greenwich
Project Overview
This research project aims to predict depression risk in university students using machine learning algorithms. By analyzing various lifestyle, academic, and environmental factors, we can identify students who may be at risk and provide early intervention support.
